About This Event

Cardz For Kidz is a nonprofit that delivers handmade cards to children in hospitals around the world.

Through this event, students will create their own cards to share encouragement and kindness. We aim to spread positivity and give back.

How to Participate

A quick guide to making and submitting your cards

1

Make Your Card

Create a handmade card using your creativity and kindness, following the guidelines.

2

Submit Your Card

Turn in your completed card to your teacher by the [March] deadline for collection.

3

Recognition & Prizes

All cards will be collected, and selected cards will receive prizes for creativity and effort.

Guidelines

1

Encouraging Messages Only

Things you can write

  • You're Awesome
  • You Rock
  • You're one in a Minion

Do not write

  • Hang in there
  • Get well soon
  • Stay Strong
  • You got this
2

Multiple Languages Needed

Because cards are sent to over 50 countries, we encourage you to make cards in different languages.

3

Sign Your Cards

Be sure to sign your cards with your first name and location
Ex: From Angie in Hacienda Heights, CA
